Damage happens in less than 0.3% of our moves — and when it does, we take full responsibility. All interstate moves are regulated by the FMCSA. By default, your shipment is covered under Released Value Protection at $0.60 per pound per article at no additional charge (the federally required minimum). For greater peace of mind, we also offer Full Value Protection, which covers the cost of repair, replacement, or a cash settlement at current market value. We file claims promptly, follow through until resolved, and never go silent after moving day.
