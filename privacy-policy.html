Privacy Policy & SMS Terms

50STATEMOVERS INC · Last updated: May 20, 2026

50STATEMOVERS INC ("50STATEMOVERS," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you interact with our website or provide your contact details to request a moving quote.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Move details such as origin state, destination state, home size, and planned move date
  • Any special notes or items you include in your quote request

We may also collect standard technical data automatically when you visit our website (IP address, browser type, pages visited) through analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Prepare and deliver your free moving quote
  • Contact you by phone, email, or SMS to discuss your move
  • Schedule, confirm, and coordinate your move
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. SMS Text Message Terms

By checking the SMS consent box on our quote form, you expressly consent to receive text messages from 50STATEMOVERS at the mobile number you provide. These messages may include:

  • Your quote details and pricing information
  • Move confirmation, scheduling, and day-of updates
  • Follow-up messages related to your move or services
  • Occasional promotional offers (infrequently and only if you remain opted in)

Message frequency: Varies. Most customers receive 2–5 messages related to their quote and move.

Message & data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

Carrier liability: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time. You will be unsubscribed immediately and will receive a final confirmation message. To re-subscribe, text START.

For help: Reply HELP or contact us at contact@50statemovers.com or (888) 505-1086.

50STATEMOVERS will not sell or share your mobile number with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., scheduling software, CRM platforms), bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Moving crew partners who assist in completing your move
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights & Contact

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, or if you have any questions about this policy, contact us:

  • Email: contact@50statemovers.com
  • Phone: (888) 505-1086

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
